Research Understudies

I love stories: writing them, reading them, abstracting them. As an undergraduate, I coded thousands of children's narratives alongside Dr. Marsha Walton for her psychological resarch in the act of storytelling as moral development. She published the results of the epic study in this book.

Facebook affords a landscape for legitimate buisnesses to operationalize their marketing tactics. Scammers operate within the same landscape. For an ongoing research project about sweepstakes and contest promotions on a Facebook group, I conducted qualitative coding and analysises on large-scale data sets. The study, conducted by Dr. Daniel Carter, aims to understand the motivations, profit mechanisms and relationships among the group's owner and its members.

How sustainable are data visualization practices? I worked with Dr. Daniel Carter to interview journalists and developers who create data visualizations. The study is not yet finished, and therefore the results are not yet published.
